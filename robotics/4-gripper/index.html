Robotic Gripper

Compact 3D-printed end-effector for basic grasping experiments.

Overview

A simple prototype made to learn Solidworks using tutorials and practice with 3D printing.

  • 3D-printed jaws with screw-driven mechanism
  • Servo-actuated open/close motion
  • Designed for lightweight objects
